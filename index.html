About Us

Complete Homes is a community-oriented association established in 2023 with the aim of supporting families and individuals with essential household items and financial assistance. We believe that a stable home environment is fundamental to personal growth and community development.

Our association was formed by a group of community leaders who recognized the growing need for accessible household essentials and emergency financial support. Since our foundation, we have helped over 500 families create complete and comfortable homes.

Through the generous contributions of our members and partners, we maintain a diverse inventory of household items that are available to our members, ensuring that everyone has access to the essentials needed for a functional home. From the profits earned, we also take care of the Elderly and The Needy in the community

We also focus on women empowerment. The program supports spiritual growth, social development, health, and financial progress. It includes physical training sessions. These sessions help women build social skills and income related skills. The goal is balanced growth across personal strength, community engagement, and economic stability.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To foster community resilience by providing essential household items and financial support to members, creating stable and nurturing home environments for all.

We accomplish this through sustainable resource sharing, financial education, and building a supportive network of community members committed to helping one another thrive.

Our Vision

A community where every household has access to the resources they need to create a complete, comfortable, and sustainable home environment.

We envision a future where cooperative resource sharing and mutual financial support eliminate barriers to essential household needs for all community members.

Our Services

Furniture Items

Access to essential furniture pieces including beds, sofas, dining tables, and storage solutions to create functional living spaces.

Kitchen Essentials

Complete range of kitchen necessities from appliances to cookware, ensuring members can prepare nutritious meals at home.

Home Appliances

Essential appliances like refrigerators, washing machines, and other time-saving devices that make home management easier.

Table Banking & Low-interest Loans

Members contribute money into a shared pool and offer each other low-interest loans. It encourages saving, builds trust, and helps families grow financially together.

Water Storage Solutions

Quality water tanks, containers, and storage systems to ensure every household has reliable access to clean water for daily needs.

Hardware & Tools

Essential hardware items and tools for home maintenance and repairs, helping members maintain safe and functional living environments.
